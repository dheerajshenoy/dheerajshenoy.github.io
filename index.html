About Me

I'm currently a PhD student in cosmology, exploring the large-scale structure of the universe and the physics that governs it. Before this, I worked at the Indian Institute of Astrophysics during my master’s thesis and internship, where I focused on solar physics — studying the Sun’s energetic phenomenon environment (CME). My journey blends deep curiosity about the cosmos with hands-on research across scales, from our star to the edge of the universe.

Projects
I like writing programs to make my life easier, better and interesting. Because of this I have written plenty of programs, created many softwares and libraries to learn or to understand how something works. I usually go with the following good (yet absurd) philosophy:
“If I see a software that does the job but could be better, I don’t wait — I build it myself. When something feels worth improving, and I can create it, I do.”
Here are few of my personal favourite project:
  • dodo: A fast and unintrusive PDF reader
  • visound: A python library for converting images to sounds in many ways
  • SonifyCPP: Program that converts images to sounds
  • Navi: Highly Customizable/Extensible file keyboard driven file manager
